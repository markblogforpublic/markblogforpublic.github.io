My favorite artist: milet

The voice that's always on my playlist.

我最爱的歌手：milet

一直在我歌单里的声音。

headphones

milet is my favorite artist. She debuted in 2019 with inside you, produced by ONE OK ROCK's Toru — and her husky, low, gravelly voice immediately set her apart. It's rare and powerful, the opposite of the typical sweet J-Pop voice.

She grew up partly in Canada, so she naturally weaves English and Japanese into her lyrics. She writes her own songs, and every track feels like a genuine story — personal, emotional, never forced. Her music blends J-Pop with rock, folk, and atmospheric indie — spacious and deeply moving.

Beyond the voice, she's just a genuinely warm and cheerful person. She named her dog Jean after her favorite painter, calls her fans "miles," and after a health hiatus in 2025, she came back saying she's learned to embrace imperfection. That honesty is what makes her so inspiring.

milet 是我最爱的歌手。2019 年以 ONE OK ROCK 吉他手 Toru 制作的〈inside you〉出道——她沙哑、低沉、颗粒感的嗓音立刻让她与众不同。那种声音稀有而有力量，和常见的甜美 JPOP 女声截然不同。

她少年时在加拿大生活过，所以能自然地把英语和日语编织在歌词里。她自己写歌创作，每一首听起来都像真实的故事——私人、真挚、毫不做作。她的音乐融合 JPOP、摇滚、民谣和氛围感独立音乐，空灵而动人。

除了声音，她本人也是一个温暖开朗的人。她以最喜欢的画家给爱犬取名 Jean，称粉丝为"miles"，2025 年因健康原因休整后复出，说学会了接纳不完美的自己。正是这份真实让人由衷喜欢。
