About Me

Hi there! I'm Mark, a developer based in Tokyo, focused on building practical open-source tools that solve real-world problems. I believe software should be useful, well-documented, and accessible to everyone.

flag Blog Goals & Directions 博客目标与方向

My projects span a range of technologies and domains. I currently work with:

  • code Languages & Frameworks — Python, JavaScript/TypeScript, Next.js, React, FastAPI, Flask
  • psychology AI & LLMs — OpenAI / Anthropic APIs, local GGUF models, prompt engineering, RAG, ASR (Whisper)
  • desktop_windows Desktop & GPU — CustomTkinter GUIs, CUDA / Vulkan acceleration, llama-cpp-python
  • description Tools & Data — PyMuPDF, MongoDB, data analytics, Recharts visualization

I'm always exploring new areas — from AI-powered education to e-commerce automation and beyond. This blog is where I document what I learn and build. Future projects will expand into more domains and technologies.
